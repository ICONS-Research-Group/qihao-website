Qihao Li

Associate Professor at College of Communication Engineering, Jilin University

Hello and welcome to my personal website! As an Associate Professor at the College of Communication Engineering, my primary research focus is on the Internet of Things (IoT), Wireless Networking, and Intelligent Agents Communication. I am passionate about exploring how these technologies can be harnessed to create smarter, more efficient communication systems. Additionally, I am dedicated to mentoring students and collaborating with industry partners to translate research into real-world applications.
