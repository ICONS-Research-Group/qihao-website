Research Areas

Internet of Things (IoT)

Our research in Internet of Things (IoT) focuses on developing innovative solutions for intelligent agent communication and networking. We explore advanced methodologies for enabling efficient and secure communication between IoT devices, with particular emphasis on:

Intelligent Agents

Development of smart agents capable of autonomous decision-making and adaptive behavior in IoT environments.

Device Networking

Optimization of device-to-device communication protocols and network architecture for IoT systems.

Security & Privacy

Implementation of robust security measures and privacy-preserving techniques for IoT networks.

Recent Work

  • Development of efficient communication protocols for IoT devices
  • Implementation of intelligent agent systems for automated decision-making
  • Research on IoT security and privacy preservation techniques
